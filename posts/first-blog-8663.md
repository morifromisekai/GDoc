Hi,

I am Yogya and I love games.

There are many things that made me fall in love with games. Most of the credit goes to the stories these games narrate. From the first cut scene to the last some of my favorite titles had me biting my nails and kicking my feet. All of these stories built in me a love for narrative design.

The only thing I remember about the first game I had ever played is what I was feeling then. It planted a seed in my head - a seed that would always draw me towards games. Ever since I can remember, I had always wanted to play and learn more about as many games as I could.

I started this place to share what I learn from video games and learn more along the way. I am currently pursuing my dream of making games that move people as much as my favorite games moved me. One step at a time. Starting with a Bachelors in Design, specializing in game design. This blog is a formal outlet for all my discoveries, learnings & occasional game reviews. I don't have a strict schedule at the moment but expect a post every week.
